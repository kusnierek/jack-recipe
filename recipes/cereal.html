Recipe for Cereal

Description

Cereal is incredibly easy to make, all you need is a few simple ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Spoon
  • Bowl
  • Milk
  • Cereal

Steps

  1. Get a bowl.
  2. Get a spoon.
  3. Get your desired cereal.
  4. Pour cereal into bowl.
  5. Pour milk over cereal.
  6. Enjoy!
